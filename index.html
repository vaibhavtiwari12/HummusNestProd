About

Welcome to Hummus Nest

Hummus nest is a Middle eastern cloud kitchen operated by Healthy Monk foods we serve different flavors of middle eastern Hummus , falafels , pita breads ,Lebanese wraps etc. We are also the first hummus specialty cloud kitchen of Central India Our mission is to give Indian public the healthier food options at the comfort of their home without any compromise with the taste. Right now we are operating our first kitchen from Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh we also have plans to expand our kitchens once our business reach a certain level of demand.

Our USPs

  • We serve middle eastern fast food which is considered as the healthiest of fast food one can have . There have been several studies done across the globe showing the nutritional value of hummus.

  • We keep inventing new recipes and add it to our menu like Chipotle & Makhani hummus is the exclusive creation of our chef .

  • We serve one of the healthiest of the wraps available in the industry with very little use of oil & unhealthy sauces.

  • Almost 80% of our menu is vegan/plant based.
